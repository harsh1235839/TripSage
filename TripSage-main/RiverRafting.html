Rishikesh Itinerary (2 Days)

Day 1: Arrival and Rafting Morning: Arrive in Rishikesh, check into your accommodation. Late Morning: Head to the river rafting starting point. Afternoon: Experience river rafting on the Ganges, navigating rapids of varying difficulty (class I-IV). Most trips last around 1-2 hours. Evening: Explore the local area, visit Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula, and enjoy a riverside café.

Day 2: Explore Rishikesh Morning: Visit the Beatles Ashram and Parmarth Niketan. Afternoon: Enjoy a relaxing yoga session or a nature walk in the nearby hills. Evening: Depart from Rishikesh.

Kullu-Manali Itinerary (2 Days)

Day 1: Arrival and Rafting Morning: Arrive in Kullu-Manali, check into your accommodation. Late Morning: Drive to Pirdi or Bhundsi, the starting points for river rafting. Afternoon: Go river rafting on the Beas River, experiencing exciting rapids and beautiful scenery. Rafting usually lasts around 1-2 hours. Evening: Explore Manali’s local attractions like Mall Road and Hadimba Temple.

Day 2: Explore Manali Morning: Visit Solang Valley for adventure activities or take a scenic drive to Rohtang Pass if it's open. Afternoon: Relax in Manali or visit nearby attractions like the Vashisht Hot Springs. Evening: Depart from Manali.
