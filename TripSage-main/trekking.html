Chadar Trek Itinerary (8-9 Days)

Arrival and Acclimatization: Arrive in Leh and spend 1-2 days acclimatizing to the high altitude. Explore Leh’s local attractions and prepare for the trek.

Travel to Trek Start Point: Drive from Leh to Chilling, the starting point of the trek. Spend the night in Chilling or nearby.

Trekking: Begin the trek and follow the Chadar (frozen river) for 5-7 days. Key camps include: Tibb Cave: A key campsite along the trek, known for its beautiful ice formations. Nyerak: A remote village offering spectacular views. Return: Trek back to Chilling and drive back to Leh. Spend 1-2 days in Leh post-trek for rest and departure.
Total Duration: 8-9 days

Roopkund Trek Itinerary (8 Days)

Arrival and Drive to Base Camp: Arrive in Delhi and drive to Kathgodam. From Kathgodam, drive to Lohajung (the base camp for the trek). Spend the night in Lohajung.

Trekking: Begin the trek from Lohajung and continue for 5-6 days, passing through: Didna Village: A quaint village with stunning views. Aali: Known for its panoramic vistas. Bedni Bugyal: A high-altitude meadow offering breathtaking views. Roopkund: The high-altitude lake surrounded by snow-capped peaks, the trek’s highlight.

Return: Trek back to Lohajung and drive to Kathgodam. Return to Delhi for departure.
Total Duration: 8 days
